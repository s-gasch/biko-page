Quick start

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Open the app & complete onboarding

    On first launch, a short 3-screen tour introduces the Dashboard, Map, and Sensors tabs. You can tap Skip anytime to jump straight in.

  2. 2

    Allow Location access

    Bike-o needs location permission to record your route and show your position on the map. Tap Allow While Using App when prompted. Without it, GPS tracking and speed will not work.

  3. 3

    Record your first ride

    On the Dashboard, tap Start, then confirm within 2 seconds. Your route, speed, and elevation are logged automatically. Tap Stop and choose Stop & Save when you're done.

  4. 4

    Connect a Bluetooth sensor (optional)

    Go to Settings → Heart Rate or Sensors and tap Scan. Select your device from the list. Bike-o supports standard BLE sensors (Garmin, Wahoo, Polar, and others).

  5. 5

    Navigate or follow a GPX route

    On the Map tab, long-press anywhere to set a destination, or import a GPX file and tap Follow Track. Bike-o will guide you back on route if you stray more than 50 m.

  6. 6

    Export your ride as GPX

    After saving a ride, tap Share as GPX on the ride summary screen to export your track to any compatible app or service.
