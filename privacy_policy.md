# Privacy Policy

**Biko — Cycling Computer**  
**Effective Date:** May 28, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 28, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Sebastian Gasch ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Biko mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Biko is a cycling computer app that displays ride metrics, records routes, and connects to Bluetooth sensors. **We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal data on external servers.** All data remains exclusively on your device.

---

## 2. Information We Access

To provide its core functionality, Biko accesses the following categories of information — all of which stay on your device:

### 2.1 Location Data

- **Purpose:** Measuring speed, distance, altitude, grade, and recording your ride route on the map.
- **When accessed:** Only while you are actively using the App or recording a ride (background location is used solely during active ride recording).
- **Storage:** Route data is stored locally on your device. It is never uploaded to any server.
- **Sharing:** You may voluntarily export your ride as a GPX file via the system share sheet. This is entirely user-initiated.

### 2.2 Bluetooth Sensor Data

- **Purpose:** Connecting to external Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) sensors — heart rate monitors, speed & cadence sensors, and temperature/humidity sensors — to display real-time ride data.
- **When accessed:** Only when BLE features are enabled by you in Settings, and only while the App is in use or recording a ride.
- **Storage:** Sensor identifiers (UUID, name) are stored locally so the App can automatically reconnect. Heart rate, speed, cadence, and temperature readings are displayed in real time and not persisted beyond the current session unless part of an active ride recording.

### 2.3 Motion & Barometer Data

- **Purpose:** Estimating altitude changes and slope grade while riding.
- **When accessed:** Only during active use or ride recording.
- **Storage:** Locally on device only.

### 2.4 Documents & Downloads Folders

- **Purpose:** Reading GPX track files that you explicitly choose to import.
- **When accessed:** Only when you initiate a file import action.

## 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

Biko does **not**:

- Collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.
- Use analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry SDKs.
- Display advertisements or use ad-tracking frameworks.
- Use the App Tracking Transparency framework (tracking is not performed).
- Create user accounts or require login.
- Access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or calendar.
- Use fingerprinting or device identification for tracking purposes.
- Share any data with third parties.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Biko uses **no** third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data. The only external service interaction is with Apple's App Store infrastructure for processing in-app subscriptions (Biko Pro). Apple's handling of payment data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

---

## 5. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Biko offers an optional monthly auto-renewable subscription ("Biko Pro"). All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

---

## 6. Data Storage & Retention

All App data (settings, sensor configurations, ride recordings, favorites) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults, temporary files). 

- **Deletion:** Uninstalling Biko removes all associated data from your device.
- **No backups to our servers:** We operate no servers or cloud infrastructure.

---

## 7. Data Security

Since all data resides solely on your device, it is protected by iOS's built-in security features, including device encryption, app sandboxing, and biometric/passcode device lock. We do not transmit data over the network, eliminating the risk of interception in transit.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Biko does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no personal data from any user, there is no specific risk to children. The App contains no user-generated content, social features, or communication channels.

---

## 9. Your Rights

Since Biko stores all data locally on your device and does not collect any data on servers:

- **Access & Portability:** You have full access to your data at all times on your device. You can export ride data as GPX files.
- **Deletion:** Delete the App to remove all data, or clear individual settings within the App.
- **Opt-out of location/Bluetooth:** You can revoke permissions at any time in iOS Settings. The App will continue to function with reduced capability.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because we do not collect or process personal data on any server, these rights are inherently satisfied — your data never leaves your device.
